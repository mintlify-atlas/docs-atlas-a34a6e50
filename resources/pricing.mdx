## Technical Implementation

<Note>
For developers and curious users: PDF AI's subscription system is built with industry-standard tools.
</Note>

### Architecture

- **Payment Processing**: Stripe API (v2023-10-16)
- **Checkout Flow**: Stripe Checkout Sessions
- **Subscription Management**: Stripe Billing Portal
- **Webhooks**: Real-time subscription status updates
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with Drizzle ORM
- **Authentication**: Clerk for secure user management

### Code References

| Component | File Location |
|-----------|---------------|
| Stripe Integration | `src/lib/stripe.ts:3` |
| Subscription Check | `src/lib/subscription.ts:6` |
| Checkout Endpoint | `src/app/api/stripe/route.ts:11` |
| Webhook Handler | `src/app/api/webhook/route.ts:11` |
| Database Schema | `src/lib/db/schema.ts:25` |
| UI Component | `src/components/SubscriptionButton.tsx:8` |

### Webhook Events

PDF AI listens for these Stripe webhook events:
- `checkout.session.completed` - New subscription created
- `invoice.payment_succeeded` - Recurring payment processed

These ensure your subscription status is always synchronized.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch between plans?">
    Yes! You can upgrade from Free to Pro at any time. The Pro features activate immediately upon payment. To downgrade, cancel your Pro subscription and you'll revert to Free at the end of your billing period.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer annual billing?">
    Currently, PDF AI only offers monthly billing at ₹1,600/month. Annual billing options may be added in the future.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?">
    We handle refunds on a case-by-case basis. Contact support within the first few days of your subscription if you're not satisfied, and we'll work with you to find a solution.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Will my price change if I subscribe now?">
    Your subscription price is locked in when you subscribe. If we change pricing in the future, existing subscribers maintain their current rate.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer discounts for students or nonprofits?">
    Special pricing for students, educators, and nonprofits is not currently available but may be offered in the future. Contact us to express interest.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I pause my subscription?">
    Subscription pausing is not currently supported. You can cancel and re-subscribe anytime, and all your data will be preserved.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel?">
    Your PDFs, chats, and account data are preserved when you cancel. You can re-subscribe at any time to regain full Pro access. If you want data deletion, contact support separately.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I pay with methods other than credit card?">
    Currently, payment is processed through Stripe which accepts major credit and debit cards. Other payment methods (UPI, net banking, etc.) may be added based on user demand.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
